# bank_churn_analysis# 🏦 Bank Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of 10,000 bank customers using Google BigQuery (SQL) 
and Power BI to identify churn drivers and retention opportunities.

**Tools:** BigQuery SQL · Power BI  
**Dataset:** 10,000 bank customers across France, Germany, Spain

---

## 🔑 Key Findings
- 📊 Overall churn rate: **20.37%**
- 🇩🇪 Germany churn rate: **32.44%** — 2x higher than other markets
- 👤 Age 46–60 churn rate: **51.12%** — highest risk segment
- 💰 Churned customers held **25% higher balances** than retained ones
- 💀 Customers with 4 products: **100% churn rate**
- 🔴 Top 20 lost customers = **£3.8M+ in deposits**

## 💡 Recommendations
1. Launch Germany-specific retention campaign
2. Re-engage inactive customers aged 46–60
3. Stop cross-selling beyond 2 products
4. Assign relationship managers to high-balance inactive accounts
